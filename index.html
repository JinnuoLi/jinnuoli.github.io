About

I will receive my Master of Information Security degree from the School of Computer Science, China University of Geosciences (Wuhan) in July 2026, and I will join the School of Cyber Science and Technology, Shandong University, as a Ph.D. student in September 2026. I received my Bachelor of Information Security degree from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in July 2023. I am very fortunate to be supervised by Hao Cheng and Weijia Wang during my time at Shandong University. My research interests focus on post-quantum cryptography, hardware-security, and side-channel attacks.
